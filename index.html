WHAT IS CHEAPPIE?

Cheappie, a start-up product, offers a melted chocolate bread snack tailored to suit local tastes. Located on ESSU MAIN campus, this product is well served by the students and business enthusiasts seeking good quality snacks at affordable prices.

Our mission is to create a welcoming business environment where customers frequently visit, relax, and enjoy our snack while watching the eye-catching beauty of the tender space that wraps around. Our primary goal is to foster strong customer relationships, enhance business recognition, and to serve a unique taste for every customer.
